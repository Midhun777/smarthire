1. Project Abstract

The Job AI Intelligent Career Platform is an advanced recruitment system that integrates artificial intelligence to facilitate optimal matching between job seekers and employers. By employing sophisticated algorithms to analyze candidate skills, experience, and preferences against job requirements, the platform streamlines the hiring process, reduces time-to-hire, and enhances the quality of matches. The system comprises three core modules: an administrative portal for system oversight, a provider interface for posting and managing vacancies, and a seeker interface for profile creation, job search, and application tracking.
